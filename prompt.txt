Details:

* Bands used: B02, B03, B04, B08

* Resolution: 10m
* CRS: UTM (auto from metadata)
* Labels: NOT available (inference only)

Processing Requirements:

* Load JP2 bands using rasterio or GDAL
* Stack bands → (H, W, 4)
* Normalize values
* Ensure alignment between t1 and t2
* Tile into 256x256 patches before inference

NDVI:

* NDVI = (B08 - B04) / (B08 + B04)
* Use NDVI for filtering vegetation changes

Important:

* Do NOT use R20m or R60m bands
* Do NOT treat files as single images
* Must combine bands manually
